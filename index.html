Zero Trust & Proactive Security

Eliminating attack surfaces and managing critical vulnerabilities

Critical Patching: CVE-2025-66478

Our team orchestrated an emergency response to a critical RCE in the Next.js RSC protocol. We identification and patched 7 production projects within 24 hours of disclosure.

# Severity: 10.0 (Critical)
Action: Upgraded to Next.js v16.0.7+
Result: Zero exploitation detected

Zero Trust Networking

We eliminated public SSH ports by implementing Tailscale. Servers are now effectively invisible to the public internet, requiring identity-based SSO for access.
